Scholarship Information

1. Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship (SC Category)

  • Eligibility: Scheduled Caste (SC) students
  • Benefits: Tuition fees, exam fees, maintenance allowance
  • Income Limit: Less than ₹2.5 lakhs per annum

2. Post-Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Tribe (ST) Students

  • Eligibility: Scheduled Tribe (ST) students
  • Benefits: Full fee reimbursement and maintenance allowance
  • Income Limit: Less than ₹2.5 lakhs per annum

3. Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC, SBC, VJNT Students

  • Eligibility: OBC, VJNT, SBC categories
  • Benefits: Reimbursement of tuition and examination fees
  • Income Limit: ₹1 lakh to ₹8 lakhs

4. Economically Backward Class (EBC) Fee Reimbursement

  • Eligibility: Open category students with income below ₹8 lakhs
  • Benefits: 50% to 100% tuition and exam fee reimbursement

5. Minority Scholarship

  • Eligibility: Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi students with income below ₹2.5 lakhs
  • Benefits: Tuition fee reimbursement, maintenance allowance
  • Scheme: Merit-cum-Means / Post-Matric Minority Scholarship

6. Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance

  • Eligibility: Rural area or economically backward students
  • Benefits: Monthly hostel allowance

7. Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Fee Reimbursement

  • Eligibility: SC, ST, OBC, VJNT, SBC in professional courses
  • Benefits: Full/Partial tuition and exam fee reimbursement

8. State Government Open Merit Scholarship

  • Eligibility: 60%+ in Class 12
  • Benefits: Fixed annual scholarship amount

9. Scholarship for Students with Disabilities (Divyangjan)

  • Eligibility: 40% or more disability
  • Benefits: Tuition waiver, exam fee, maintenance allowance

10. Government Hostels / Student Welfare Facilities

  • Eligibility: Students from rural, tribal, and backward areas
  • Facilities: Subsidized accommodation, mess, study support

Important Notes:

  • Apply online via MahaDBT Portal
  • Aadhaar card and bank account are mandatory
  • Submit all required documents before the deadline
